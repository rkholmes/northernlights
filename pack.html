Don't forget!

We do our best to provide our customers with everything they might need during their trips, but there are some extra items that you might want to bring with you. Recommended items vary depending on the vacation package, but we’ve included some of the most common items in the table below.

Hotels will often provide basic toiletries such a soap, shampoo, conditioner, and lotion, but we still recommend packing your favorite toothbrush and any personal bathroom items that you might miss while you’re away. Also, be sure to bring any medications that you might need, as well as any necessary medical equipment, such as a CPAP machine or syringes for insulin. If you are planning for a cruise and you are prone to seasickness, we suggest bringing dramamine. The waters are typically smooth, but it never hurts to be prepared.

If you are a history buff, we recommend investing in a guidebook before you embark on your journey in Sweden. We recommend the Lonely Planet series, as they come with easy-to-read maps and informative descriptions of landmarks. Finally, we recommend sturdy hiking boots and other outdoorsy items for our wilderness retreat in Norway, and we recommend a bathing suit for the Icelandic spa, although they are available at the resort for a nominal fee.
