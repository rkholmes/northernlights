Nice to meet you!

Northern Lights was founded as a labor of love by Jan and Erika Hansen in 2012. As travel enthusiasts, they have traveled to 14 countries and counting. When they began suggesting travel itineraries to friends, Northern Lights was born. As Nordic natives, they are passionate about bringing the gems of their homeland to a wider audience. They are proud to have facilitated dozens of satisfying trips to northern Europe, and they hope to continue bringing joy for years to come!

Since their founding in 2012, Northern Lights has been dubbed Best Travel Agency by Akron Life Magazine and has been featured in many local publications, including the following:

  • Akron Beacon Journal
  • The Plain Dealer
  • Cleveland Scene

Jan and Erika are happy to answer any questions you may have about vacation packages, travel services, financing, or logistics. Just use the form on the Contact Us page!
